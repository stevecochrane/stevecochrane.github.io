Services

Different jobs, different skills, consistent results.

I write across a range of formats, from websites and articles to white papers, press releases and longer pieces of content.

Some briefs need a high impact commercial tone. Some call for structure, research and depth. And sometimes I take existing content and strip it back until it says exactly what it’s supposed to say.

Different formats but the same job: write something that works for the intended audience (and sometimes that means just stating the obvious).

01

Website copy

Clear writing that cuts through with elegant simplicity. Let’s people know who you are, what you do and why they need you.

  • Homepages and landing pages
  • Service and product pages
  • About Us pages and wider brand messaging
  • Reworking or tightening existing copy

02

Articles and blog writing

Blog posts, features and articles written to inform, explain or persuade.

  • Business blogs and think pieces
  • Editorial comment
  • Complex issues and stories turned into accessible content
  • SEO content that remains readable
  • Ghostwritten content for brands and individuals

03

White papers and long-form content

Longer work calling for in-depth research, structure, clarity and impeccable sourcing. Useful for establishing your voice as an authority in a particular sector.

  • White papers
  • Reports and data-led pieces
  • Guides and longer downloadable content
  • E-Books

04

Technical Content

Detailed technical material taken and shaped into readable content in the form of web pages, articles or long-form content. From in-depth legal coverage to industrial processes.

  • Websites
  • Sales brochures
  • Blog posts

05

Promotional content

The right words to sell what you want to sell quickly and effectively.

  • Social media posts
  • Promotional brochures
  • Landing pages
  • Product descriptions

06

Scriptwriting

Having written for the stage, TV and radio I know the difference between the spoken and printed word. I can create or polish copy so that it sings when lifted off the page.

  • Broadcast-related writing
  • Script development
  • Spoken word formats
  • Information with a conversational tone

How I work

I work with you to establish what it is you need the writing to do, whether we’re working from scratch or with existing content. Sometimes the client provides a brief. Sometimes rough notes or a first draft. Often it’s simply a quick chat or email.

The point is to come up with the aim of the work, the intended audience, and the tone of voice.

With this done we can agree a timetable and cost based on the length and detail of the writing and the amount of research and development involved.

The simple version
